Whey Protein Isolate

Essential

💡 Purpose

Supports muscle recovery and growth. High-quality protein source with rapid absorption for post-workout nutrition.

📊 Dosage

25-30g per serving
1-2 servings daily depending on dietary protein intake

⏰ When to Take

Post-workout (within 30 minutes) and/or between meals as needed to meet protein targets

⚠️ Warnings

Avoid if lactose intolerant. Choose isolate for better digestion. Check for allergens.

⚡

Creatine Monohydrate

Performance

💡 Purpose

Enhances strength, power output, and muscle endurance. One of the most researched and effective supplements for athletes.

📊 Dosage

5g daily
Maintenance dose (no loading phase needed)

⏰ When to Take

Post-workout with carbohydrates for better absorption, or any time of day consistently

⚠️ Warnings

Stay well hydrated. May cause minor water retention. Consult doctor if you have kidney issues.

🐟

Omega-3 Fish Oil (EPA/DHA)

Health

💡 Purpose

Reduces inflammation, supports heart health, improves joint mobility, and aids recovery from training.

📊 Dosage

2-3g EPA+DHA daily
Check label for combined EPA/DHA content

⏰ When to Take

With meals for better absorption. Can split dose between morning and evening meals.

⚠️ Warnings

Choose high-quality, tested brands. May thin blood - inform doctor before surgery.

☀️

Vitamin D3

Essential

💡 Purpose

Supports bone health, immune function, muscle function, and overall athletic performance. Many athletes are deficient.

📊 Dosage

2000-4000 IU daily
Consider testing blood levels for optimal dosing

⏰ When to Take

Morning with breakfast and a source of fat for better absorption (fat-soluble vitamin)

⚠️ Warnings

Very high doses can be toxic. Get blood levels checked. Works best with adequate calcium and K2.

🌙

Magnesium Glycinate

Recovery

💡 Purpose

Improves sleep quality, reduces muscle cramps, supports recovery, and helps manage stress and anxiety.

📊 Dosage

300-400mg elemental magnesium
Glycinate form for better absorption

⏰ When to Take

Evening, 30-60 minutes before bed for optimal sleep and recovery benefits

⚠️ Warnings

Some forms may cause digestive upset. Glycinate is gentler. Avoid if you have kidney disease.
