Q&A of the Day

Q: Should I focus on fat loss or weight loss?

Understanding the difference is key to reaching your fitness goals. Weight can fluctuate for various reasons (water, muscle, etc.), but fat loss is a more gradual and sustainable change.

Q: How much sleep should I be getting?

For athletes, 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night is recommended. Does quality matter? YES. To maximize recovery and performance, quality sleep is non-negotiable.

Q: How can I improve my reaction time?

Reaction time improves with consistent sleep, proper hydration, and targeted drills (like ball-drop, sprint-start, or decision-making exercises). Short, focused sessions 2-3 times per week can sharpen speed and accuracy over time.
